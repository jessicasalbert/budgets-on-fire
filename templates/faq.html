    NO SUCH THING AS A SILLY QUESTION

    What's 'FIRE'?

    'Financial Independence Retire Early' is the idea that you can buy freedom by saving enough money such that you no longer depend on a 9-5 to support yourself.

    How much should I be saving?

    The simple answer: as much as you can! We like to aim for a 50% savings rate, but any amount of saving is better than none.

    What do I invest in?

    We recommend broad-based index funds, which you can 'set and forget.' Avoid actively managed funds with high fees which will eat into your earnings.

    Roth vs. Traditional?

    Roth: pay taxes now.
    Traditional: pay taxes later.
    Consider your current tax rate and how much you plan to spend in retirement.

    What's my 'FI number'?

    The 4% rule of thumb suggests that you've reached FI when you have saved 25x your annual expenses. You can withdraw 4% each year and maintain your principal investment.

    ...but I like my job?

    We emphasize the "FI" rather than the "RE". FIRE simply gives you options and the freedom to choose. If you choose to continue work, that's awesome too!
